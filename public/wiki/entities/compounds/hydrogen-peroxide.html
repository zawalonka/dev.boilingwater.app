Hydrogen Peroxide

Compound

Educational notes

Boiling point 150°C - much higher than water! This is surprising given H₂O₂ is so similar to H₂O. Decomposes at high heat into 2H₂O₂ → 2H₂O + O₂ (this is why it foams on wounds - catalase enzyme speeds decomposition, releasing oxygen bubbles). Store in dark containers - light accelerates decomposition.
