Sucrose

Compound

Educational notes

Does not have a normal boiling point - decomposes into caramel before boiling! Melts at 186°C. When heated, sucrose breaks down into glucose + fructose, then further decomposes into hundreds of flavor/color compounds (caramelization). Solubility in water increases dramatically with temperature (180g/100mL at 0°C → 487g/100mL at 100°C).
