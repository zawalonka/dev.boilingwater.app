Methane

Compound

Educational notes

Boiling point -161.5°C - methane is a gas at all normal Earth temperatures! Must be cooled to -161.5°C or compressed to liquefy. Simplest hydrocarbon (one carbon atom). Main component of natural gas (~95%). Major greenhouse gas (25× more potent than CO₂ over 100 years).
