Educational notes

Hafnium is refractory metal - melting point 2,233°C! Hafnium alloys used in nuclear reactor control rods. Hafnium carbide has one of highest melting points of any compound. Hafnium oxide used in semiconductor gate insulators (advanced chips). Discovered in 1923. Named after Hafnia (Latin for Copenhagen). Hafnium-tungsten alloys resist extreme temperatures. Rocket nozzles use hafnium alloys. Expensive and specialized.
