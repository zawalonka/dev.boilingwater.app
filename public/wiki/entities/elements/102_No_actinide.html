Educational notes

Nobelium is named after Alfred Nobel - inventor of dynamite! Nobelium-259 half-life 58 minutes. Synthesized in 1966 in accelerators. Chemical identification challenging - only few atoms. Decay and chemistry inference. Named controversially (priority dispute). Element properties largely theoretical. Femtogram quantities. No applications. Research element only.
