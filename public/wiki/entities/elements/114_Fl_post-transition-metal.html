Educational notes

Flerovium is named after Flerov Laboratory - Russian nuclear research center! Flerovium-289 half-life 2.7 seconds. Synthesized in 1999. Transactinide - predicted lead-like. Super-heavy chemistry barely explored. Few atoms produced. Named in 2012. Heavy element physics frontier. Research element only.
