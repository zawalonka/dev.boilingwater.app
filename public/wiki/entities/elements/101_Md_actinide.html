Educational notes

Mendelevium is named after Dmitri Mendeleev - creator of periodic table! Mendelevium-258 half-life 51 days. Alpha emitter. Produced via heavy-ion accelerator. Named in 1955. Only handful of atoms produced - studied individually. Absorption, decay patterns measured. Transuranium element - only in reactors. Femtogram quantities produced. Theoretical chemistry interest.
