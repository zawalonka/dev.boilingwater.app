Educational notes

Vanadium is a 'wonder metal' - adds strength to steel without making it brittle! Adding just 0.1% vanadium to steel increases hardness dramatically. Discovered in 1801. Named after Vanadís (Norse goddess of beauty). Vanadium compounds used as catalysts in industrial chemistry. Vanadium redox batteries store large amounts of electrical energy - potential breakthrough for renewable energy storage. Some organisms concentrate vanadium from seawater.
