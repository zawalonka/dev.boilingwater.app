Nitrogen

Element · N

Educational notes

Essential for all life - key component of proteins and DNA! Makes up 78% of Earth's atmosphere as N₂ gas, yet most organisms can't use it directly (nitrogen fixation by bacteria converts it). Discovered in 1772 by Daniel Rutherford. Liquid nitrogen is extremely cold (-196°C) and used for cryogenic preservation. Nitrates and nitrites are crucial plant nutrients but excess runoff causes algal blooms. Nitrogen cycle: atmosphere → soil → plants → animals → back to atmosphere.
