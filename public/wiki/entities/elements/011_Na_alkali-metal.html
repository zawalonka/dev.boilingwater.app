Educational notes

Sodium metal reacts explosively with water - burns with yellow flame! Despite this, sodium ions (Na⁺) are essential for nerve signals, muscle contraction, and blood pressure regulation. Discovered in 1807 by Humphry Davy through electrolysis. Table salt (NaCl) is ~39% sodium - too much sodium in diet increases heart disease risk. Sodium vapor lamps (street lights) emit characteristic yellow glow. Used in thermal energy storage systems and liquid metal reactor cooling.
