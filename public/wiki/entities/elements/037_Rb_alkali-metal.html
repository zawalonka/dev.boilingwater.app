Educational notes

Rubidium is highly reactive alkali metal - stored under mineral oil like sodium! Rubidium-87 is naturally radioactive with 49-billion-year half-life. Cesium-rubidium atomic clock is one of world's most precise instruments. Rubidium compounds used in photomultiplier tubes (detect faint light). Fireworks burn rubidium red (like potassium burns lilac). Rubidium is rare and expensive. Discovered in 1861 by spectroscopy - first element discovered this way.
