Carbon

Element · C

Educational notes

The element of life - basis of all organic chemistry! Forms more compounds than any other element (>10 million known). Exists as graphite (pencil 'lead', soft), diamond (hardest natural material), graphene (strongest material known), and fullerenes (soccer-ball molecules). Plants use carbon dioxide + sunlight to make glucose (photosynthesis). Fossil fuels (coal, oil, gas) are ancient compressed carbon-rich organisms. Carbon-14 dating measures age of archaeological finds by radioactive decay (half-life 5,730 years).
