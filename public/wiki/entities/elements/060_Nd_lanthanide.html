Educational notes

Neodymium is essential for strong permanent magnets - your headphones likely contain neodymium! Nd-Fe-B magnets are strongest permanent magnets known - used in electric car motors, wind turbines, hard drives. Neodymium laser used in surgery and industry. Discovered in 1885. Neodymium oxide gives purple color to glass. China controls 90% of neodymium supply - creates supply chain vulnerability. Critical element for green energy transition.
