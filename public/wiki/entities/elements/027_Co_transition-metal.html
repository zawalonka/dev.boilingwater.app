Educational notes

Cobalt is the blue color in ceramics and glass - medieval blue pottery used cobalt oxide! Cobalt-60 is radioactive isotope used in cancer radiotherapy and food sterilization. Cobalt-blue pigments remain vibrant for centuries - priceless in paintings. Vitamin B12 contains cobalt at its center - critical for nerve function and DNA synthesis. Cobalt mining is controversial (child labor in Congo). Ferromagnetism: cobalt magnets are incredibly strong.
