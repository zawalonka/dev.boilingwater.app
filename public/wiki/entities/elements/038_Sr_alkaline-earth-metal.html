Educational notes

Strontium burns with brilliant crimson flame - gives red color to fireworks! Strontium-90 is radioactive fission product of nuclear reactors - absorbed by bone (health hazard). Strontium compounds (strontium carbonate) produce red color in fireworks. Strontium ranelate is drug that strengthens bone (used for osteoporosis). Discovered in 1790. Celestite (strontium sulfate) is beautiful blue mineral. Strontium isotopes tracked by scientists to date geological samples.
