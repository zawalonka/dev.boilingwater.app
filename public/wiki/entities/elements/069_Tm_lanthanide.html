Educational notes

Thulium is rarest lanthanide - most expensive rare earth after francium! Thulium-170 X-ray source for portable radiography (field use). Thulium-doped fiber laser produces infrared. Thulium is toxic and accumulates in body. Blue thulium oxide used in high-quality glass. Discovered in 1879. Named from Thule (ancient name for Scandinavia). Limited applications due to scarcity and cost. Research interest in medical imaging.
