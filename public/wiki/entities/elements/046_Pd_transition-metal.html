Educational notes

Palladium is amazing catalyst - speeds up hydrogen addition to molecules! Car catalytic converters need palladium (cleaner than rhodium alone). Hydrogen can dissolve in palladium - once thought possible energy storage. Palladium jewelry is hypoallergenic alternative to gold. Palladium compounds used in organic chemistry reactions. Discovered in 1803. Named after asteroid Pallas. Supply from Russia/South Africa can create supply crunches.
