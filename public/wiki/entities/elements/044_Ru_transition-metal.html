Educational notes

Ruthenium is platinum-group metal - extremely hard and corrosion-resistant! Ruthenium-platinum alloys used in jewelry (hypoallergenic). Ruthenium catalysts speed up industrial chemical reactions. Osmium-ruthenium alloys are hardest metals known. Ruthenium dioxide used in electrodes. Named after Ruthenia (Latin name for Russia). Discovered in 1844. Expensive precious metal but less than platinum. Used in solar cells and electronics.
