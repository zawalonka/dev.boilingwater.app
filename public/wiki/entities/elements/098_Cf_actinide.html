Educational notes

Californium has interesting fission properties - californium-252 spontaneously fissions (neutron source)! Used in well logging (oil drilling), medical diagnostics, nuclear reactor startup. Californium-252 extremely valuable - $27 million per gram! Named after California/University of California. Discovered in 1950. Only nanogram quantities accessible. Most dangerous actinide by weight. Killed scientist who dropped microscopic amount.
