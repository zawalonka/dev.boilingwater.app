Element · I

Educational notes

Iodine is essential for thyroid function - your thyroid gland produces hormones regulating metabolism. Iodine deficiency causes goiter (swollen thyroid) and brain damage in children - adding iodine to salt prevented this in developed countries. Iodine-131 is radioactive product of nuclear reactors - accumulates in thyroid after releases (Chernobyl, Fukushima). Iodine solution is used as disinfectant for wounds. Purple iodine vapor is used in security marking (appears when pressure applied). Discovered in 1811.
