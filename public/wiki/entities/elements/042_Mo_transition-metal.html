Educational notes

Molybdenum is essential trace element in humans - deficiency is rare but serious! Molybdenum enzymes break down purines and detoxify harmful compounds. Tool steel with molybdenum is extremely hard - used for drill bits and cutting tools. Molybdenum disulfide is dry lubricant (better than oil in many applications). High melting point (2,623°C) - used in furnaces. Molybdenum-99 produces medical imaging agent technetium-99m. X-ray tube anodes use molybdenum.
