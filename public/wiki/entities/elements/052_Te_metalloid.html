Educational notes

Tellurium is rare metalloid - selenium's heavier cousin! Tellurium dioxide used in optical fibers for telecommunications. Cadmium telluride solar cells are efficient photovoltaics. Tellurium alloys strengthen steel and copper wires. Tellurium compounds have garlic smell - tellurium breath odor lasts weeks! Discovered in 1782. Produced as byproduct of copper/gold mining. High refractive index used in lenses. Brittle crystalline element with metallic luster.
