Educational notes

Copernicium is named after Nicolaus Copernicus - father of heliocentrism! Copernicium-285 half-life 29 seconds. Synthesized in 1996. Transactinide - predicted mercury-like. Flash chemistry studies performed. Named in 2010. First element named after person (Copernicus). Extremely short-lived. Research element.
