Element · Tl

Educational notes

Thallium is highly toxic - historically used in rat poison (now banned)! Thallium poisoning causes hair loss, nervous system damage. Despite toxicity, thallium-201 medical imaging detects heart disease. Thallium compounds have low melting point - used in glass/lenses. Thallium sulfide used as infrared detector. Discovered in 1861 via spectroscopy. Name from Greek 'thallos' (green shoot) - spectral line green. Bioaccumulates in plants.
