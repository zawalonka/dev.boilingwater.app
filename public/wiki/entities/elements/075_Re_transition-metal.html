Educational notes

Rhenium is extremely rare and expensive - cost ~$20,000 per kilogram! Rhenium-molybdenum alloys used in rocket engines (Space Shuttle). Rhenium-osmium alloy has incredibly high melting point. Rhenium catalysts used in petroleum refining. Discovered in 1925 (one of last naturally occurring elements). Named after Rhine River. Rhenium-188 radioactive for cancer therapy. Limited supply - mostly byproduct of molybdenum mining.
