Educational notes

Polonium is extremely radioactive - most dangerous element by weight! Single microgram kills you. Polonium-210 used in nuclear weapons detonators. Polonium used as neutron source in nuclear bombs. Polonium-alpha emitter - irradiates everything nearby. Named after Poland (discovering element Marie Curie). Polonium spiked tea kills KGB defector Litvinenko (2006) - polonium dust invisible killer. Exists only in radioactive form.
