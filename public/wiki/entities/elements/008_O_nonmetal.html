Oxygen

Element · O

Educational notes

You breathe 550 liters of oxygen every day! Discovered in 1774 but Earth's early atmosphere had none - cyanobacteria produced it over billions of years (Great Oxygenation Event 2.4 billion years ago). Makes up 21% of air, 46% of Earth's crust (in rocks/minerals), and 65% of your body by mass. Supports fire - wood won't burn in pure nitrogen but blazes in pure oxygen. Liquid oxygen is pale blue, magnetic, and used as rocket fuel (paired with liquid hydrogen in space shuttles).
