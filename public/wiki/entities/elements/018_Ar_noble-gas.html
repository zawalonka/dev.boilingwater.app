Educational notes

Argon makes up 0.93% of Earth's atmosphere - more abundant than oxygen-18 or other trace gases! Inert noble gas - doesn't react with anything. Used in light bulbs and fluorescent tubes (glows red/purple). Argon ion lasers used in eye surgery. Welding uses argon as inert atmosphere to prevent oxidation. Liquid argon used in cryogenic applications. Argon dating measures age of rocks - atoms of radioisotope argon-40 accumulate in volcanic rock over time.
