Element · Br

Educational notes

Bromine is the only liquid element at room temperature (besides mercury) - orange liquid with acrid smell! One of rarest elements. Bromine bromide compounds used in photographic film (now declining with digital cameras). Flame retardants contain bromine - slows fire spread but some banned due to health concerns. Seawater contains bromide - extracted industrially. Bromine is toxic and corrosive - chemists respect it. Discovered in 1825.
