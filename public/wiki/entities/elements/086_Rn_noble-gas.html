Educational notes

Radon is radioactive noble gas - seeps from rocks and accumulates in basements! Radon-222 decays to polonium - second leading cause of lung cancer (after smoking). Radon test kits detect accumulation. Ventilation removes radon gas. Radon daughter products (polonium, bismuth, lead) stick to lung tissue - causes damage. Discovered in 1900. Named from Latin 'radius' (ray). Only element that's gas at room temperature.
