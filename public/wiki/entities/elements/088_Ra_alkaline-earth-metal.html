Educational notes

Radium glows in dark - early watch faces painted with radium paint (workers died from radiation)! Radium-226 found in uranium ores. Marie Curie isolated radium, won Nobel Prize, died from radiation. Radium-223 used in cancer treatment (targets bone tumors). Alpha emitter - internals safe, external dangerous. Radon produced from radium decay. Used in old lightbulbs and clock dials (now banned). Extremely toxic.
