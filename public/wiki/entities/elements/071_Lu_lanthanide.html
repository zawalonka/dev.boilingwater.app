Educational notes

Lutetium is the last lanthanide (chemically) - heaviest rare earth at 71! Lutetium-175 stable, lutetium-176 radioactive. Lutetium compounds in high-temperature ceramics. Lutetium tantalate crystal used in phonon engineering. Discovered in 1907. Named from Lutetia (Latin for Paris). Lutetium-177 used in cancer therapy (radioimmunotherapy). Expensive and limited applications. Extremely dense element.
