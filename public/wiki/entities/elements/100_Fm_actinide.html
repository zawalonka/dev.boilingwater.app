Educational notes

Fermium is named after Enrico Fermi - physicist who built first nuclear reactor! Fermium-257 half-life 100 days (longest-lived). Alpha and beta emitter. Produced in particle accelerator collisions. Named in honor but discovered via bomb fallout. Picogram quantities produced. Extremely costly - single atom production feasible. No practical applications. Pure research interest.
