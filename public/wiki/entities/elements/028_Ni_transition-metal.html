Educational notes

Nickel makes stainless steel stainless - without it, steel rusts! Nickel-cadmium batteries were first rechargeable batteries (now replaced by lithium). Nickel plating prevents corrosion - coins contain nickel (some people allergic to nickel earrings). Nickel-iron meteorites formed Earth's core. Thermal vents in the ocean have nickel-rich minerals. Nickel catalysts used in industrial hydrogenation (making margarine from oils).
