Educational notes

Antimony is toxic element - used in flame retardants and old medicines! Antimony trioxide fire retardant slows burning (now restricted in some uses). Antimony compounds were used in ancient kohl eyeliner. Antimony telluride thermoelectric material converts heat to electricity. Antimony trisulfide is bright orange pigment. Battery alloys contain antimony for better charging. Discovered in ancient times. Named from Arabic 'stimmi'. Biased between metal and nonmetal properties.
