Educational notes

Cesium is the most electropositive element - reacts explosively with water! Burning cesium burns brilliant blue/violet. Cesium-137 is radioactive fission product (Chernobyl contamination). Cesium atomic clock is world's primary time standard - defines the second. Cesium formate used as drilling fluid in oil/gas wells. Discovered in 1860 via spectroscopy. Named from Latin 'caesius' (sky blue). Liquid near room temperature (melts at 28.4°C).
