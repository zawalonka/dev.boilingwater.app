Educational notes

Barium compounds glow under UV light - used in old radar screens and X-ray imaging! Barium sulfate (insoluble) swallowed for GI tract X-rays - shows digestive system clearly. Barium carbonate used in glassmaking and ceramics. Barium peroxide releases oxygen when heated. Barium is highly reactive alkali earth metal. Discovered in 1808. Soluble barium salts are toxic - cause severe poisoning. Barytes (barium sulfate mineral) extremely dense - used as drilling mud.
