Educational notes

Platinum is most precious metal - doesn't tarnish or corrode ever! Ancient Egyptians used platinum 3,000 years ago. Platinum catalytic converters reduce car pollution (also recycled for value). Platinum jewelry never needs polishing. Platinum resistance thermometers measure temperature precisely. Chemotherapy drug cisplatin uses platinum. Discovered in South America by Spanish conquistadors. Price fluctuates but always expensive.
