Educational notes

Essential for life - DNA and ATP (energy molecules) contain phosphorus! Discovered in 1669 by phosphorescent glow. White phosphorus ignites spontaneously in air and produces thick white smoke (used in fireworks and military smoke screens). Red phosphorus is stable and used in safety matches and explosives. Phosphorus-32 is radioactive and used to label biological molecules in research. Fertilizers add phosphate - critical for global food production but causes water pollution (algal blooms).
