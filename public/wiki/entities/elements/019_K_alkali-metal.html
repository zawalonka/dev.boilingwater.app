Educational notes

Potassium is critical for nerve signals and heart function - athletes drink sports drinks with K⁺ to replace losses. Bananas are famous for potassium (though yogurt has more per serving!). Potassium metal is silvery and must be stored under mineral oil because it reacts violently with air and water - burns with lilac flame. Discovered in 1807 by electrolysis. Potassium-40 is naturally radioactive and causes all humans and bananas to be slightly radioactive. Used in fertilizers.
