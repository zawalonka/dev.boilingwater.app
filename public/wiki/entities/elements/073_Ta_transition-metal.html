Educational notes

Tantalum is highly corrosion-resistant - used in electronics and surgical implants! Tantalum capacitors store energy in phones, computers, radios. Body accepts tantalum implants for bone repair (hypoallergenic). Tantalum carbide extremely hard - used in cutting tools. Discovered in 1802. Named after Tantalus (mythological figure). Tantalum mining (coltan) controversial in Congo (blood tantalum). Electronics industry biggest consumer.
