Helium

Element · He

Educational notes

Second most abundant element in the universe! Only element that doesn't solidify when cooled (stays liquid at absolute zero under normal pressure). Discovered in the Sun's spectrum in 1868 before being found on Earth. Helium balloons float because He is 7× lighter than air. Used in MRI machines (liquid cooling), deep-sea diving (replaces nitrogen to prevent 'the bends'), and as inert atmosphere for welding. Helium-3 isotope used in lunar regolith research.
