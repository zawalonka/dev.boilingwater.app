Educational notes

Promethium is rare - only radioactive rare earth element without long-lived stable isotope! Promethium-147 emits beta particles used in nuclear batteries (power remote sensors). Self-luminous promethium paint on watch dials glows without charging. Discovered in 1945 in nuclear reactor fission products. Named after Prometheus (stole fire from gods). Artificial element produced in reactors. Very rare and toxic. Medical research uses pm-147.
