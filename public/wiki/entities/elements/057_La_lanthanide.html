Element · La

Educational notes

Lanthanum starts the rare earth element series! Lanthanum oxide used in camera lenses (high refractive index). Lanthanum nickel metal hydride batteries power hybrid cars. Mischmetal (lanthanum-cerium mixture) ignites spark plugs. La-Mg alloys are super-strong and lightweight. Rare earth magnets contain lanthanum. Discovered in 1839. Despite 'rare' name, more abundant than tin/lead. Lanthanum carbide is hard ceramic material.
