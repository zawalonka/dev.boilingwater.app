Educational notes

Germanium was predicted by Mendeleev before discovery - showed the periodic table's power! First semiconductor used in transistors (1950s) before silicon took over. Germanium detectors sense radiation in nuclear physics. Optical fibers for internet use germanium compounds. Germanium dioxide used in catalysts and optical materials. Rare element - extracted as byproduct of zinc and copper mining. Similar properties to silicon but heavier.
