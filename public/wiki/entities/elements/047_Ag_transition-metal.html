Educational notes

Silver is the best conductor of electricity and heat - but copper is cheaper so copper wires dominate. Silver has antibacterial properties - used in wound dressings and water filters. Mirrors use silver coating because it reflects 95% of light. Photography used silver halide (AgBr) in film - chemicals change with light exposure. Silver jewelry and tableware last centuries. Silver-110m is radioactive and used in medical imaging. Discovered and valued since ancient times.
