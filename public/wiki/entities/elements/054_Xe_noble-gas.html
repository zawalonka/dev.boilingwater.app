Educational notes

Xenon is noble gas - element's electrons so tightly bound it doesn't react! Yet xenon fluoride compounds exist under extreme pressure. Xenon arc lamps produce bright white light (used in cinema projectors). Xenon anesthetic is safer than halothane for some surgeries. Xenon-135 poisons nuclear reactors (absorbs neutrons). Xenon is rare - extracted from air after nitrogen/oxygen/argon removed. Discovered in 1898. Most expensive noble gas.
