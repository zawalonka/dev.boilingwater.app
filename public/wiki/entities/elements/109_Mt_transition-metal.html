Element · Mt

Educational notes

Meitnerium is named after Lise Meitner - physicist who explained nuclear fission! Meitnerium-278 half-life 7.6 seconds. Synthesized in 1982. Transactinide element. Predicted to resemble iridium. Few atoms - chemistry theoretical. Named in 1997 (honoring her contributions to nuclear physics). Research element. No applications.
