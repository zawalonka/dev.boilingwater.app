Element · Yb

Educational notes

Ytterbium has unique electronic structure - used in atomic clocks! Ytterbium optical lattice clocks are extraordinarily accurate (better than cesium). Ytterbium-doped fiber lasers produce powerful infrared. Ytterbium-2 (divalent) shows unusual quantum properties. Discovered in 1878. Named after Ytterby. Ytterbium accumulates in bone tissue. Space-based ytterbium clocks enable GPS and relativity tests.
