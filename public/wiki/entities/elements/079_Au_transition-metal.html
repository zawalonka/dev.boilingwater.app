Educational notes

Gold doesn't tarnish or corrode - jewelry from 3000 years ago looks new! Symbol Au from Latin 'aurum'. Most precious metal, prized for wealth and beauty. Chemically unreactive - won't react with oxygen, water, or most acids (only aqua regia dissolves it). Gold is extremely dense - one ounce of gold can be pounded into a sheet covering 300 square feet. Gold-198 is radioactive and used in medical imaging. All gold ever mined could fit in a cube 22 meters on a side.
