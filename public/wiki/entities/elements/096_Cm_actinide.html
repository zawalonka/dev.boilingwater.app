Educational notes

Curium is named after Marie Curie - honors her radioactivity research! Curium-244 half-life 18 years. Alpha emitter - glows visibly from self-heating! Curium heat generators power space probes. Neutron source for nuclear detectors. Americium-241 decays to curium-241. Discovered in 1944. Extremely radiotoxic. Silvery metal when purified. Medical uses in targeted therapy research.
