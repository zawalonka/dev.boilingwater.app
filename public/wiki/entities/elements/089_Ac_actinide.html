Educational notes

Actinium is radioactive - starts the actinide series! Actinium-227 used in medical research. Actinium glows faintly in dark (alpha radiation excites surrounding material). Extremely rare - only found in uranium ores. Named from Greek 'aktis' (ray). Discovered in 1899. Actinium-225 experimental cancer therapy. Chemical behavior similar to lanthanum. High specific radioactivity despite long half-life.
