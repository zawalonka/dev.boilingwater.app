Magnesium

Element · Mg

Educational notes

Burns with brilliant white light - used in flashbulbs and fireworks! Lightest structural metal (used in airplane fuselages to reduce weight). Magnesium sulfate (Epsom salt) soaks relieve muscle aches. Chlorophyll (the green pigment in plants) has magnesium at its center - without it, photosynthesis fails and Earth would starve. Discovered in 1808. Magnesium batteries are being researched as safer alternatives to lithium-ion. Body needs ~400mg daily for muscle function.
