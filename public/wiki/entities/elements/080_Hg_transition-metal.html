Element · Hg

Educational notes

Mercury is the only liquid metal at room temperature - it's a shimmering silver liquid that rolls into spheres. Extremely toxic - mercury vapor damages brain and kidneys. Thermometers used mercury for 300 years (now mostly replaced with alcohol). Mercury switch lights toggle when tilted - old devices had them. Used in gold mining (amalgam extraction) causing severe poisoning in artisanal miners. Dental amalgams (silver fillings) contain mercury (controversy over safety). Named after Roman god - symbol Hg from Latin 'hydrargyrum' (liquid silver).
