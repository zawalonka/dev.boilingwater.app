Educational notes

Astatine is rarest naturally occurring element - only ~1 gram exists in Earth's crust at any time! Element-211 synthesized in particle accelerators. Astatine-211 experimental cancer therapy (alpha emitter targets tumors). Extremely radioactive - decays quickly. Named from Greek 'astatos' (unstable). Only discovered in 1940 in cyclotron. Properties largely theoretical - too radioactive to study. Medical potential but challenging to use.
