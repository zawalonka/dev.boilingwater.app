Educational notes

Neptunium is first synthetic transuranium element - created in particle accelerators! Neptunium-237 relatively long-lived (2 million years). Found in trace amounts in uranium ores (from neutron capture). Named after Neptune (planet beyond Uranus - element beyond uranium). Discovered in 1940. Neptunium-239 decays to plutonium-239. Studied for nuclear fuel and weapons research. Extremely toxic and radioactive.
