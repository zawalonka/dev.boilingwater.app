Educational notes

Tin is oldest metal worked by humans - bronze (tin + copper) started Bronze Age! Pewter (mostly tin) makes decorative items. Tin cans (steel coated with tin) preserve food. Solder (lead-tin alloy) joins electronic components - lead-free solder now standard. Tin oxide is white pigment. Tin whiskers: spontaneous whisker growth on tin coatings causes electronics failures. Discovered since ancient times. Two allotropes: white (conductive) and gray (nonconductive).
