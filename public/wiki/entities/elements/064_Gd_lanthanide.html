Educational notes

Gadolinium is paramagnetic - attracted to magnets more than iron! Gadolinium contrast agents used in MRI - makes images clearer. Gadolinium-157 has huge neutron absorption - used in nuclear reactor control rods. High Curie temperature - used in magnets. Discovered in 1880. Named after gadolinite mineral. Gadolinium iron garnet used in microwave devices. Interesting magnetic properties from unpaired electrons.
