Educational notes

Cadmium is toxic heavy metal - causes kidney disease and brittle bones! Cadmium-nickel batteries (now largely phased out for lithium). Cadmium sulfide bright yellow pigment in paint (now restricted). Cadmium exposure from smoking and industrial pollution. Accumulates in kidneys and liver. Banned in EU electronics. Yet cadmium telluride solar cells are efficient and cheap (concerns about recycling/disposal). Discovered in 1817.
