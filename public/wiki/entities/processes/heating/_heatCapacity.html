STUB: Heat Capacity Formula (Q = mcΔT)

This process uses the heat capacity equation to calculate how much

energy is needed to change a substance's temperature, or how much

the temperature changes when energy is applied.

Purpose in this process: Calculate temperature increase from burner

energy input, and energy needed to reach boiling point.

@see ../../formulas/heatCapacity.js for the actual implementation
