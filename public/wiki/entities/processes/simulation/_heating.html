STUB: Heating Process

This process uses the heat application to convert burner power (Watts)

into temperature change and/or vapor generation.

Purpose in this process: Apply the energy from the burner each timestep.

Energy = Power × Time, then apply to fluid.
@see ../heating/applyHeat.js for the actual implementation
