STUB: Newton's Law of Cooling Formula

This process uses Newton's Law of Cooling to equilibrate the fluid

temperature toward ambient when the burner is off.

Purpose in this process: Make the fluid cool down realistically

when not being heated. Hot fluids cool faster than warm ones.

@see ../../formulas/newtonCooling.js for the actual implementation
