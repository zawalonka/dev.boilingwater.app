STUB: Ebullioscopy (Boiling Point Elevation) Formula

This process uses the ebullioscopy equation (ΔTb = i × Kb × m) to calculate

how much dissolved solutes raise the boiling point of a solution.

Purpose in this process: Apply the final adjustment for solutions like

saltwater, sugar water, etc. Pure solvents have zero elevation.

@see ../../formulas/ebullioscopy.js for the actual implementation
