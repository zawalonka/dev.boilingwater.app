STUB: ISA Atmosphere Formula

This process uses the ISA (International Standard Atmosphere) formula

to convert altitude in meters to atmospheric pressure in Pascals.

Purpose in this process: Calculate what pressure the liquid experiences

at the given altitude, which determines its boiling point.

@see ../../formulas/isaAtmosphere.js for the actual implementation
