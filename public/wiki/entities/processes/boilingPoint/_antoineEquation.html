STUB: Antoine Equation Formula

This process uses the Antoine equation to determine the temperature

at which a substance's vapor pressure equals atmospheric pressure.

Purpose in this process: Convert atmospheric pressure (from ISA) into

the base boiling point temperature for the pure solvent.

@see ../../formulas/antoineEquation.js for the actual implementation
